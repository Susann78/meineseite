Throughout +15 years as a designer I have worked with different types of clients - from large companies, to small brands and artists. Here I describe my specialties, my working progress and how they might benefit you and your business.

01

Strategy

If branding, visual identity, web design, editorial - the possibilities are unlimited, but they all begin somewhere. After various meetings defined by a barrage of questions to capture the spirit of the project's future and to understand what to do verbally, visually and strategically the short and long term, I'll build the visual strategy specifically for you.

02

Visual Identity

After diving into the project's soul through our briefing sessions and define the visual strategy, it's time to build the most appropriate visual identity for your company, by keeping adaptation for all digital and physical materials in mind. I design graphic solutions to communicate a clear message and help clients stand out from the crowd increasing their commercial success.

03

Web Design

For the development of a strong digital presentation, experience and intuitive design are crucial. With more of 15 years experience in web design and as a web developer in future ;) I create modern and responsive websites, which speak to a wide range of clients. Every big step in the creation of the project will be presented in stages, allowing each development element to be authorized as the project progresses.
